Who We Are

Founded by Isoko Natives in the USA

Isoko Health Foundation, Inc. is a Florida Nonprofit corporation founded in 2012. We are organized exclusively for charitable, medical, and educational purposes.

Our first Health Fair was held in May 2012 at Oleh General Hospital and Ozoro General Hospital, serving both Isoko North and Isoko South Local Government Areas.

Our Mission

To enhance healthcare resources and maximize their availability to the Isoko community.

Our Impact

Providing general check-ups, dental care, and optometry services to thousands of citizens.
