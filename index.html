About IHF

Founded by Isoko Natives in the USA

Isoko Health Foundation, Inc. is a Florida Nonprofit corporation founded in 2012. We are organized exclusively for charitable, medical, and educational purposes.

Our first Health Fair was held in May 2012 at Oleh General Hospital and Ozoro General Hospital, serving both Isoko North and Isoko South.

Our Mission

To enhance healthcare resources and maximize their availability to the Isoko community.

Our Programs

Comprehensive healthcare services delivered directly to the villages.

General Health Fairs

Our core program involves setting up temporary clinics in schools and town halls. We perform triage, check vitals, and provide consultations with doctors.

  • Blood pressure & sugar screenings
  • Malaria & Typhoid treatment
  • Free medication dispensary

Dental Services

Led by volunteer dentists, we provide critical dental care that is often inaccessible in rural areas.

  • Extractions and pain relief
  • Cleaning and hygiene education
  • Oral health screenings

Maternal & Child Health

We provide specialized care and education for mothers and infants to reduce mortality rates.

  • Prenatal counseling
  • Distribution of delivery kits
  • Infant nutrition support
